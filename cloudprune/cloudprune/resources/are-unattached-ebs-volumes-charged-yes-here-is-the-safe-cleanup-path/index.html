Impact and rollback

  1. Classify whether the action can affect production traffic, data retention, compliance, or incident response.
  2. Prefer dry-run review first. For storage deletion, create or verify a snapshot/export when rollback matters.
  3. Schedule changes with an owner and a validation window. Stop if the blast radius is unclear.

Questions people ask

Are available EBS volumes charged in AWS?

Yes. The available state means the volume is detached, not free. AWS still bills the allocated storage until the volume is deleted.

Can I recover a deleted EBS volume?

Only if you have a snapshot, backup, export, or another copy of the data. Deletion removes the volume, so rollback planning needs to happen before cleanup.

How should CloudPrune users review this recommendation?

Review the volume ID, size, age, tags, snapshot status, recent attachment signal, and owner notes before approving deletion or keeping the volume.
